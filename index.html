Echoes of Tomorrow: A glimpse in the future.

A science fiction story where a teenager encounters a time traveler, learning profound lessons about choices and their consequences.

Ethan, a 15-year-old who meets a time traveler named Lyra from the future. Together, they learn that small choices in the present can drastically shape the future. As they navigate pivotal moments in time, Ethan realizes that while they can't change everything, the key is making thoughtful decisions now and learning from past mistakes to create a better tomorrow. The story emphasizes the power of personal responsibility and integrity in shaping the future.

About the Authors

We are a team of storytellers who share a passion for crafting meaningful and thought-provoking narratives. The "Echoes of Tomorrow: A glimpse in the Future." was born from our collective belief that fiction can do more than just entertain, it can impart wisdom and inspire personal growth.
